🌲 I’m a nature guide, storyteller, and adventure seeker. I believe that every hike is a chance to connect with the world around us and with each other.

If you're here, chances are you're a nature lover like me. I’ve been hiking since I was five, and ever since then, the world has been my playground. I’m originally from Germany, raised by travel-hungry parents who passed their wanderlust on to me and my brother (he’s the journalist in the family – always chasing stories, while I chase sunrises from mountaintops).

I love to combine movement, connection, and discovery. That’s why I guide people like you—curious souls who want more than just a trip. You want stories to tell, and I want to help you live them.

One unforgettable moment? Swimming in a crystal-clear jungle lake deep in Brazil with a group of first-time adventurers. We laughed, we explored, and we felt alive.

🏜️ I’ll never forget our sunrise hike through Chile’s Atacama Desert. The air was still, the landscape endless—just sand, sky, and silence. As we climbed a rust-colored ridge, the sun slowly lit the dunes in soft gold. Among us was a couple on their very first hike. When we reached the top, they stood quietly, holding hands, completely in awe. It was one of those moments where time paused. That’s what I love most—when nature humbles and connects us in the simplest, most beautiful way.

🌍 I’m here to help you find your own unforgettable moments. Whether you’re a seasoned hiker or just starting out, I’ll guide you through breathtaking landscapes, share stories of the land, and help you connect with nature and each other.

My Tours

I keep groups small—2 to 6 people—so that we can move freely, connect deeply, and adapt as we go. Whether we’re summiting peaks or wandering through ancient forests, we’ll always go at a pace that feels natural.

Expect a blend of challenge, freedom, and joy. You’ll eat well, sleep comfortably, and laugh often. Some hikes are bold and daring; others are slow and soul-soothing.

For the parents—especially solo parents—I’ve designed tours that are fun, safe, and memory-packed for both you and your kids. We build confidence, make friends, and connect with nature in playful ways.
